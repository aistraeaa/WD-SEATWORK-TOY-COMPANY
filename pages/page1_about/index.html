Meet the Characters

Explore the unique personalities of PPULBATU characters.

CHOI YONG MEONG

represents SOOBIN

Species: Human
- His hobby is playing games and wants to become a game character. He always looks tired because he stays up all night playing games.
- He is an archer so he tends to carry around a bow and arrow.
- His favorite colors are black, white, and gray.
- He tends to be lethargic and quiet, but his personality changes when he puts on a mask or character.

HWANG CHOON

represents YEONJUN

Species: Fennec Fox
- He loves food and tends to get hangry if he doesn't eat.
- He enjoys rapping and even gets angry when he does it.
- His favorite colors are red and blue.
- His charming point is his difficult personality.

BAMGEUT

represents BEOMGYU

Species: Half dog, half bear
- He loves sleeping, always ready to go to bed. He tends to lie down anywhere and falls asleep as soon as his head hits the pillow.
- He loves listening to music and can play the guitar.
- He is a cheerful individual. He loves bringing smiles to people.
- He gets easily distracted, so he cannot focus on one thing.

DA-GO-NYANG

represents TAEHYUN

Species: Cat
- He loves working out and eating sweets, especially chocolate cornets.
- He is emotionless and only brightens up if something's interesting.
- His favorite color is black.
- He is a scaredy-cat and sweats a lot when he's nervous, almost to the point of a waterfall.

HMM NYA RING

represents HUENINGKAI

Species: Angel
- He likes egg bread and enjoys quiet places and being at home.
- The reason why he has one wing is because he lost it when he fell from the sky.
- His favorite colors are black, white, and purple.
- He is a very shy person, especially around new people. Depending on his mood, his hoodie can change expression.
